At Trading Mentorship Program, we help you overcome your challenges and maximize your trading potential. Having trained over 2800+ traders, we are sure that the tools & techniques designed will help you emerge as a trader who can trade in any market condition.

360 Overview

Gain experiential learning in Equity, Options, Commodity & Currency markets. This will prepare you to navigate periods of high volatility without suffering significant losses. By trading across different asset classes, you enhance your portfolio's diversification and increase the likelihood of successful outcomes.

Our mentors will also guide you to understand what type of asset class you want to trade in & what kind of trader you intend to become.

2+2+2 Program

In the first 6 months, each mentor will train you to trade in your respective areas. They will equip you with essential skills like stock selection, risk and money management, entry points, position sizing, partial profit booking, and re-entry strategies.

Over the next 6 months, you get additional hand-holding over the community to practice trading & resolve your queries under the guidance of the mentors.

1:1 Counseling Session

Throughout the program, you will get a chance to have 30-minute one-to-one counseling sessions with 3 different mentors. You can avail this advantage between the 4th and 12th month of your program duration.

All-Season Trader

We will empower you to trade and develop discipline & patience, under any given market scenario. The program focuses on intraday and swing trading across Indian and international markets. With active guidance and real-time insights, you will develop the right approach and mindset to consistently succeed in trading.

Your journey for the next 6 months

Over the next 6 months, you'll dive deep into various trading concepts, develop practical skills, and gain confidence in your trading abilities.

Week 1 - Week 6 Understanding Technical Analysis

Mentor: Souradeep Dey

Understanding Market Structure, Candlesticks, Technical Theories, Trading Masterclass, Indicators, Trend Indicators, Momentum Indicators, Volatility Indicators, and Volume Profile

Week 7 Defining RS Theory

Mentor: Vivek Bajaj

Strength Theory and RS Theory Masterclass

Week 8 - Week 12 Options Strategies

Mentor: Chetan Panchamia

Options Trading, Introduction to Derivatives, Learning Options Greeks, Options Strategies, Options Repairs and Adjustment, Max Pain Theory and Trading Rules

Week 13 - Week 16 Mastering Currency & Commodity Trading

Mentor: Saanando Das

One Good Trade and Grading Concept, Multi-asset portfolio creation, Intermarket Analysis, Basics of Derivatives, Profitable Crude Oil Trading Strategies, Commodity Fundamental including Gold, Silver + Crude Oil and Natural Gas, Trading Strategy with 3 Horses Method, Base Metals and Sentiment Analysis

Week 17 - Week 25 Understanding Trading Strategies

Mentor: Abhijit Paul

Trading Strategies and time Frames, Ingredients of our Trading Setups, Relative Strength Index Demystified, Trading Masterclass, Breakout Strategy, Contrarian Strategy, and PullBack Strategy

Convocation

One-on-one interaction with the mentors and wrapping the program by sharing each others learnings.

Access to StockEdge Social

Access to StockEdge Social, our community of over 15,000 traders, where you can discuss ideas and strategies with peers and domain experts. Its the perfect platform to level up your networking with other traders.
